About

Who I Am

Yasith Danthasinghe is a Network Operations Center (NOC) Engineer at People's Leasing and Finance in Sri Lanka. He specializes in monitoring and maintaining enterprise network infrastructure, ensuring stable connectivity across branch networks and critical financial services.

Yasith's work involves real-time network monitoring using tools like Grafana and Itheon, troubleshooting TCP/IP connectivity issues, and responding to network incidents to maintain service uptime.

Yasith holds a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology (Hons) in Computer Networks and Telecommunications from the University of Kelaniya, which laid a strong academic foundation for his career in networking and telecommunications. With multiple CCNA certifications from Cisco Networking Academy and hands-on experience with FortiManager security appliances, he brings both technical depth and operational discipline to every shift.
